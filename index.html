The Process

How it works

Ordering your dream cake or treat box is simple. Here's how we make it happen.

1

Get in Touch

Send us a message or fill in our order form with your occasion, date and ideas. We'll get back to you quickly.

2

Pick Your Options

Choose your sponge flavour, fillings and share any design inspiration — photos, colours, themes, we love it all.

3

Confirm & Pay

We'll confirm your order details and secure it with a payment. Pay online by card or opt for pay on collection.

4

Collect & Enjoy

Pick up your freshly baked creation from our Dublin location and enjoy every single bite.
